Forro at The Lighthouse
Every Tuesday! Workshops focus on footwork/roots from 7.15pm and cost £10, then there is social dancing and party until 10:45pm, and the entrance is free!
The Lighthouse Bar, 62 Rivington Street, EC2A 3AY







Forro in Juju's Bar
Wondering what to do on a boring Wednesday night? Come dance some forró!
Different level classes are usually ran from 7pm to 8:15pm with Alexandra, Anax, Cleiton and team (or occasional guest teachers). Entry £8 class included, DJ and live music after the class.
Juju’s Bar and Stage – Ely’s Yard, 15 Hanbury Street, E1 6QR London




Forro in Floripa
On special Sundays afternoons, it is time for a fantastic Fusion of Fun, Food & Forró with Friends and Family.
This takes place about twice a month. Floripa, 91-93 Great Eastern St, London EC2A 3HZ
