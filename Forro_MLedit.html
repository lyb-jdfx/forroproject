This is Forró, a Brazilian dance. Forró now is very much international, hip-swivelling, dance floor-filling, popular music and dance originated in Brazil’s north-eastern states. As some forrozeiros (fans of forró) would put it, “it’s less competitive than salsa and less pedantic than tango…”. It is arguably one of the easier partner dances to master, which would explain why it’s so addictive and increasingly popular! Oh, and by the way – it’s pronouced “faw-HAW”…
